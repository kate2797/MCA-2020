Task 2: Generating a piano roll and a pitch histogram

To complete the second task, I worked with Python library called Music21 and generated two visualisations. The graph on the left (Piano Roll) visualises how pitches evolve over time whereas the graph on the right (Pitch Histogram) visualises the count of different pitch classes.
