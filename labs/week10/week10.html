Week 10: Audio similarity and transcription

Task 1: Similarity matrix

To complete the first task, I computed a similarity matrix. First, I needed to replace country music with electronic music as that is the genre I am working with in this project. For that two more electronic songs were added (EE7A – Fairground, Synapsis – Seven). For both the rock and classical genre, I replaced one of the examples provided with the songs from the previous weeks. In the matrix, tracks 0-3 are classical, 4-6 are rock and 7-9 are electronic. There is a clear separation between the classical and rock genre which is indicated by the different-coloured squares. Interestingly, one of the electronic tracks (track 8) is very similar to the classical tracks, with roughly 0.5 similarity score. Overall, if we look at the matrix we can see that the genre of rock and electronic seem to more similar to each other than electronic and classical music.


This plot nicely visualises what has been said in the previous paragraph. One of the electronic songs appear in close proximity to the classical genre cluster. I listened to it out of curiosity and it indeed was a rather slow and less loud electronic track.
